## Design Notes
- **Primary color:** Orange/Amber (#E8860C / #F5A623 range)
- **Typography:** Mix of serif (headings) and sans-serif (body) — likely Playfair Display + a clean sans like Outfit
- **Overall feel:** Warm, cultural, inviting — balances performing arts prestige with retail accessibility
- **Key brand element:** The Star bird/wing logo icon used as a recurring motif
